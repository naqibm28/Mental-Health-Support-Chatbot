What is this?

Your Personal Mental Health Support Chatbot.

Designed to be a non-judgmental companion, this AI-powered tool helps users manage stress, anxiety, and depression through empathetic conversations, mindfulness exercises, and coping strategies. By providing a safe space for individuals to express their feelings, the chatbot bridges the gap in mental health care, especially for those who might find it difficult to seek help in traditional ways.
